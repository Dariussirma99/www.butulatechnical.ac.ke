WELCOME TO THE INSTITUTE OF EXCELLENCE

Butula Technical and Vocational College (BTLTVC) is a public TVET institution situated in Bujumba Village, Bujumba Location, Marachi West ward, Butula Sub-county in Busia County. The college was mentored by Kisiwa Technical Training Institute (KTTI). The college became effectively operational in January 2023 and currently has an enrolment of 530 trainees. The college has since been registered by the requisite bodies including KATTI, HELB, KUCCPS, TVETA, TVET/CDACC and KNEC.
